SOBRE NOSOTROS


4G Logistics is dedicated to the international transportation of fresh produce. Launched in 2009, 4G Logistics was created as an effort to deliver fresh produce just-in-time and improve communications between our legal and the logistics companies.

By having one point of contact and an unrestricted flow of information, our integrated legal and logistics services ensure that your product is delivered in a quick, traceable, and efficient manner.

Our friendly security and staff is directly connected with Interlace and can be reached 24/7. We closely monitor the entrance and departure of trucks, truck yard and surveillance system Our truck drivers can be located via GPS and contacted at any point.
